About The Platform

Smart Academic Data Cleaning Portal

A focused platform to improve academic data quality before reports, analytics, and compliance submissions.

What Is Data Cleaning?

Data cleaning is the process of detecting and fixing inaccurate, incomplete, duplicate, or inconsistent records. In academics, this includes correcting roll numbers, course codes, attendance entries, grades, and date formats so data becomes reliable and usable.

Why Academic Institutions Need It

  • Prevents reporting errors in accreditation and government submissions.
  • Improves trust in dashboards, rankings, and institutional analytics.
  • Reduces manual correction effort for staff and department teams.
  • Ensures fair decision-making based on accurate student records.
